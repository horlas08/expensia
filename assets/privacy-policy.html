🔐 Privacy Policy

DebtPro | Diouni — Last updated: January 2026

DebtPro | Diouni respects user privacy and is fully committed to protecting personal data.

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information with third parties.

All debt-related data, transactions, reminders, and contact details are entered voluntarily by the user and are used solely within the app for personal debt management and organization purposes.

Features such as friendly reminders, communication with related parties, or phone calls are initiated manually by the user. The app does not send automatic messages or perform any communication without user interaction.

Friendly reminder messages may include a short identifying phrase such as “Sent via DebtPro | Diouni”. This option can be enabled or disabled at any time through the app settings.

The app does not record calls, monitor messages, access unauthorized data, or use any information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Certain permissions (such as contacts or phone access) may be requested strictly to enable user-selected features and for no other purpose.
